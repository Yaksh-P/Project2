  • Facts of S.V.Patel

Patel was one of Mahatma Gandhi's closest allies and played a significant role in the Indian independence movement. He organized peasants in Gujarat for non-violent civil disobedience against the British.

After independence in 1947, Patel was instrumental in the political integration of India. He persuaded over 500 princely states to accede to the Indian Union.
